The Office

The Office is a sitcom that follows an ordinary paper company called Dunder Mifflin. The main characters are Jim and Dwight which are salesmen at the company. Pam the receptionist and Michael the boss.

Reasons to watch

  • The main plotline is about the relationship between Jim, the saleman, and Pam, the recptionist. It's a slow burn and well made to make you want to keep watching as theyre friendship grows throughout the show.

  • The humour. Honestly the best part of the show is how funny it is. I've seen every episode atleast 5 times and I laugh every time.

  • The show is made in the format of a documentary, making it feel natural and easy to watch.
